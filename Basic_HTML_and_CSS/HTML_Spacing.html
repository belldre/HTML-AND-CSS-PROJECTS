Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth has ordered the Navy to take the rare step
of renaming a ship, one that bears the name of a gay rights icon, documents and sources show.

 Military.com reviewed a memorandum from the Office of the
Secretary of the Navy -- the official who holds the power to name Navy ships -- that showed
the sea service had come up with rollout plans for the renaming of the oiler ship USNS Harvey Milk.

 A defense official confirmed that the Navy was making preparations to strip the ship of its name
but noted that Navy Secretary John Phelan was ordered to do so by Hegseth.
The official also said that the timing of the announcement -- occurring during Pride month -- was intentional.
